Sushi Volcano

Description

A raw fish explosion.

Ingredients

  • Prepared sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Assorted fresh fish (tuna, salmon, etc.)
  • Sushi roll fillings (e.g., avocado, cucumber)
  • Wasabi, pickled ginger, and soy sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare sashimi and nigiri.
  2. Arrange maki rolls, nigiri, and sashimi on a large platter.
  3. Serve with wasabi, ginger, and soy sauce.
