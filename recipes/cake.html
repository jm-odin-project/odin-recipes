Rainbow Unicorn Cake

Description

A psychedelic cake.

Ingredients

  • Cake ingredients (flour, sugar, eggs, butter, milk)
  • Rainbow gel food coloring
  • Buttercream frosting
  • Lots and lots of edible glitter

Steps

  1. Mix cake batter, making six bowls of different colors, and bake into layers.
  2. Stack cooled layers, adding frosting between each.
  3. Put lots and lots of glitter everywhere.
